Teacher's Day Celebration
The knowledge we earn and wisdom we learn is imparted by our Gurus, our teachers, who unveils the hidden capabilities within each and every student. The CSI Council expressed their sincere gratitude and deep respects by organizing an event for the very first time in the history of the college, as a tribute to their teachers, their mentors of life. They embraced the divinity of this bond.
The event uncovered with lightening of lamp and students expressed how thankful they were to have a guiding front man as him. The event paved its way through a short welcome speech by the Director Sir, who expressed the joy he felt, by this touching concern and warmth of the students. He ignited the spark of confidence in each student, through his well-crafted words.
Following him next was a song in mellifluous voice of Sakthi Balan sir. He sang a song in Carnatic music and not only was it soothing but also depicted that the teachers too are filled with hidden surprises.
Following them with a unique idea cherished with a nostalgic cherry, a skit was performed by the Sankalp Club. The skit imparted a wonderful message in a subtle manner to each and every person seated over there. It taught us the importance a human can gain through a perfect supervision of a Guru. The confidence that we are boosted with through this light, breaks us apart from the shackles of our own conservatism.
Without losing the excitement of the event, to continue it forward, a group of teachers came up on stage and gave song performances. Indeed, it was equivalent to going down the memory lane of their own college times. The musical performance, lightened the mood and instigated a blithe among the teachers and the students. Through their song they elaborated how they miss their childhood lives and their teachers, which were the best days one could ever have.
To add onto the beautiful moments, Beena Gokhale ma’am was invited on stage, for the much awaited performance. She had beautifully written a poem showcasing the love and affection that is instilled with deep respect, between a student-teacher relationship. Her poetry was indeed mesmerizing in words and deep in interpretation, an inspiring set of words for the audience.
A short exciting quiz was also conducted, for pouring a fun and informal taste to the event. Each teacher was asked to pen down their most beautiful moment in the college and then the papers were shuffled among all. The other teacher, marked the moment out of a score of 20. The best ones, were then later read out aloud to all, which mainly comprised of ‘this very moment’ taglines and ‘the moment they acquired a position of being someone’s teacher’.
Lined up after the quiz was a closing speech by Dr. Gorthi Sir, who saluted the efforts of the CSI Council and wished them the best of their lucks and motivated them to have faith in self, for attainment of their dreams. High tea was organized after this and on a cheerful note the event was ended with balloon blasting activity!
As a token of reverence, the CSI Council members handed over mementos to each teacher. The teachers bestowed their good wishes to the entire student community and were overwhelmed with such an affection. The bond, on this day, got stronger.
