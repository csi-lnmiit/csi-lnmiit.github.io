About Us

csi chapter lnmiit
Computer Society of India Student Chapter at the LNM Institute of Information Technology, Jaipur was established in March 2013 under guidance of Asst. Prof. Vikas Bajpai. CSI student chapter of LNMIIT is a open forum that helps students through various programs like quizzes, talks, discussions, workshops and other events.
